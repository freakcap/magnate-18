Beta-Pitch

Beta-Pitch is an event where you put forth your ideas and give it a test to how much it matters in the real world. This is a part of a bigger platform where you will be given a chance to present your idea, explain it, and put it out to test its applications.
About the event:

  1. STAGE 1: Preliminary Round – Brief your idea
    1. Send your idea in brief. Should contain-
      • Name
      • Problem Solution
      • Market analysis
      • Unique selling point
      • Team
    2. Your idea will be evaluated by judges’ short listing the top teams who will be eligible for the next stage.
  2. STAGE 2: Day of the event- Presentation
    • Each team will be given 15 minutes to present your ideas live at the workshop (warning bell at 10 and 13 minutes).
    • You need to convince the judges and the crowd why you think the people will invest in your ideas.
    • You also need to talk about the pros and the cons of your idea.
    • The presentation will be followed by Q&A session.
    • The judging is going to be done by investors/professionals from the concerned field.

Rules and Regulations:

  • No limit to number of candidates per team.
  • Candidates pursuing undergraduate, graduate, post graduate programs can participate.
  • The proposed idea should be suitable for funding at some level to take it further.
  • The proposal of the idea should not be in violation of any intellectual property rights as far as you are aware.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and shall be respected.

Judging Criteria:

  • Strength of your team.
  • Creativity or how innovative your idea/concept is.
  • Originality
  • Implementation
  • Understanding of competitors.
  • Feasibility of the idea/product/business venture.

MAD ADS

This event is specifically leans towards testing your marketing skills. An advertisement should be as good as its product it portrays. The skill of persuasion for making a consumer buy the product is what marketing Is all about. How the product caters to the needs of the consumer and highlighting its pros is what you need to do here. Rules and Regulations:

  1. Each participant will be given a product. You need to advertise it in such a way so that the consumer is compelled to buy the product.
  2. You will be given some time to think about it.
  3. And you have to present it in the best way possible.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Based on the Creativity of your ad.
  2. How you optimize the resources around you to make your point.
  3. The language used and the way you present it.
  4. How compelling your ad is to the consumer.
